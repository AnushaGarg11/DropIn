Understanding Career Counseling

Career counseling is a vital resource for students who feel lost or uncertain about their future plans. It provides guidance to help students identify their strengths, interests, and potential career paths, ultimately assisting them in making informed decisions about their future.

Why Career Counseling Matters

Many students drop out of school due to a lack of clear future plans or because they feel disconnected from their educational goals. Career counseling can address these issues by:

  • Helping students explore various career options based on their skills and interests.
  • Providing information on educational and training opportunities.
  • Offering support and motivation to help students stay engaged in their studies.
  • Assisting in setting realistic and achievable career goals.
