Free Resources

  • SWAYAM: A government initiative offering free online courses from various Indian universities and institutions.
    Visit SWAYAM

  • National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL): Free engineering and technical courses available for students across India.
    Visit NPTEL

  • Khan Academy: A global non-profit offering free courses in subjects like mathematics, science, and economics.
    Visit Khan Academy

  • edX: Offers free courses from universities worldwide, with the option for paid certification.
    Visit edX

  • Coursera (Free Courses): Although Coursera offers paid courses, many are also available for free.
    Visit Coursera

  • FutureLearn: Offers free courses on a wide range of subjects including healthcare, business, and creative arts.
    Visit FutureLearn

  • Alison: Provides free courses in IT, healthcare, business, and more, with certifications available for a fee.
    Visit Alison

  • Udemy (Free Courses): Offers both paid and free courses in areas like programming, business, and personal development.
    Visit Udemy Free Courses

  • MIT OpenCourseWare: Access free courses, lectures, and assignments from one of the world's top universities.
    Visit MIT OpenCourseWare

  • OpenLearn by The Open University: Free courses across a wide range of disciplines, offered by The Open University.
    Visit OpenLearn
