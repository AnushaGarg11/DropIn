Government Scholarships :

These scholarships, offered through platforms like the National Scholarship Portal, cater to various categories such as economically weaker sections, SC/ST students, minorities, and differently-abled individuals. They aim to reduce financial barriers by covering tuition fees, living expenses, and even stipends. The government's focus on providing scholarships ensures that talented and deserving students have access to quality education, regardless of their financial background.

Some of the them are listed below:

  • National Means Cum Merit Scholarship (NMMS)

    • This government-funded scholarship is targeted at economically weaker students in Class 8 to prevent them from dropping out after Class 8. It provides a fixed annual financial grant to continue their education till Class 12.

  • Indian Oil Academic Scholarships

    • Indian Oil Corporation offers scholarships to economically disadvantaged students pursuing higher education, particularly in engineering, medical, and MBA fields. The scholarship is awarded based on merit and need.

  • Swami Vivekananda Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship

    • This West Bengal government scholarship provides financial support to economically disadvantaged but meritorious students from Classes 11 and 12, aiming to reduce school dropout rates due to financial struggles.

    Website: Link

  • SJVN Silver Jubilee Merit Scholarship Scheme

    • SJVN Limited, a government-owned hydroelectric power company, offers scholarships to meritorious students from economically weaker sections pursuing higher education in various fields, including engineering, medicine, and humanities. It focuses on students from Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Bihar, and Maharashtra.

    Website: Link

  • Dr. Ambedkar Post-Matric Scholarship for Economically Backward Classes (EBC)

    • This scholarship assists economically backward class (EBC) students at the post-matric level. It covers tuition fees, maintenance allowances, and other academic costs, helping students from low-income families continue their education.

    Website: Link

  • SAIL Merit Scholarship

    • Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) provides scholarships to meritorious students from economically weaker backgrounds pursuing education in schools and professional degrees like engineering and medical.

    Website: Link

  • Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme (PMSSS)

    • The PMSSS is aimed at students from Jammu and Kashmir and Ladakh who wish to pursue undergraduate courses in colleges outside their home region. It provides financial assistance for tuition fees, hostel charges, and other educational expenses.

Private Scholarships :

These scholarships, provided by corporations, non-profit organizations, and educational institutions, are often aimed at recognizing and rewarding excellence in academics, sports, and other fields. These scholarships are highly competitive and are typically merit-based, though some also focus on financial need. Many private scholarships offer mentorship programs, internships, and networking opportunities, helping students not only financially but also in career development.
